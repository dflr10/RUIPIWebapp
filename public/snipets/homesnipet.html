IDENTIFICATION AND REGISTER APP RUIPI


"Contribuyendo con la mejora en la atención en salud de las comunidades indígenas de la Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta"


Faced with the current crisis of COVID 19, it is clear that all institutions must rethink the way in which they have offered their services and products. If we go to the realm of health, we find that, in the context of the most vulnerable populations, including the indigenous population, it is much more tangible and evident the negative impact it has had on the economic, social, mental and cultural spheres the present problem. Now, with the arrival of this crisis, the areas of systems have also evolved, in order to respond with effective solutions to the problems caused by the pandemic.



Business RUIPI Aplication

The customized software for the registration and identification of indigenous patients solves the characterization problem motivated by the lack of an identity document of a effective and simple way. With this system, medical care processes are improved. In addition to this, RUIPI offers the option of managing the clinical history and generating a pdf copy to be downloaded.

Who developed this project?

The developers of the RUIPI system are technologists in analysis and development of information system. Both are dedicated to the study and design of software and websites.
